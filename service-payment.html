Pay securely with escrow.

Your funds are held safely by an independent third-party escrow partner until the work is completed.

How escrow protects you
Your payment is not released to the service provider immediately. It is held by an independent escrow partner while the work is being done. Once you confirm that you are happy with the completed service, the funds are released to the provider.

Booking summary

SkillBridge deducts a 20% platform fee from the provider’s payout after successful completion. You will only release funds after the work is done and you approve it.
