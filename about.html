Our purpose

Opportunity should be within reach.

We are creating the bridge between South African skills, ambitious businesses and the customers who need them.

More than a marketplace

A digital ecosystem for an economically stronger South Africa.

SkillBridge gives every skilled individual an opportunity to earn, every business affordable ways to market and grow, and every student a better path to internships and employment.

Customers can search trusted service providers, compare businesses, read reviews, make bookings and pay securely. Businesses can build profiles, advertise their work, recruit staff and access growth support.

Students can showcase their skills, qualifications and portfolios, then apply directly for internships and employment opportunities.

As SkillBridge grows, we aim to partner with universities, TVET colleges, government departments, private companies and funding organisations. Our long-term goal is to expand across Africa as a trusted hub for skills, services, entrepreneurship and employment.
